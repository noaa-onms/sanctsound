Software

  • Marina: Matlab GUI that houses all apps by the Passive Acoustic Research Group at the Northeast Fisheries Science Center
  • Marine Bioacoustics Research Collaborative
    • Triton: Triton with Remoras in development
      • Soundscape Metrics: Calculation of binned power spectral density and band level products.
      • Logger: Manual logging of acoustic events.
      • Airgun-Detector: Detection of airgun shots in decimated acoustic data.
      • Explosions-Detector: Detection of explosions in decimated acoustic data.
      • Blue Whale Detector: Detection of blue whale north-eastern Pacific B calls
      • Fish Detector: Detection of bocaccio calls
      • GPLReview: A graphical user interface for evaluating and editing acoustic detections (mostly used for low-frequency data).
    • DetEdit: A graphical user interface for annotating and editing events detected in long-term acoustic monitoring data
