Killer whales in OCNMS

Sights & Sounds

Killer whales are highly vocal using sound to assist in navigation, communication and searching for prey. Recording these vocalizations can help us better understand when and how endangered Southern Resident killer whales are using the sanctuary. Photo: OCNMS, NOAA
